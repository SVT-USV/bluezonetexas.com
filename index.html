Our Story
Fair pay. Real transparency. From day one.

Founded in January 2026, Bluezone Consulting Services was born out of frustration with an industry that puts its own profits ahead of people. Traditional consultancies routinely take 30–50% of an employee's bill rate — we believe that's wrong.

Our model is simple: we keep only 15–20%, and the rest goes to you. In just 5 months, we've already placed 5+ professionals in meaningful roles — and we're just getting started.

  • Only 15–20% commission — you keep what you earn
  • Transparent contracts with no hidden deductions
  • 5+ successful placements since January 2026
  • Employee-first culture at every step
  • Equal Opportunity — diversity and inclusion at our core
